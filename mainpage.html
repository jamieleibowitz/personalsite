About Me

Hello! My name is Jamie, and my pronouns are she/her or they/them. I graduated in 2017 from Northwestern with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. As an Engineering student, I worked with places such as the Adler Planetarium, Schmoop, and Fermi National Laboratory.

I am currently in the market for web developer jobs as well as positions that utilize my knowledge of maker technologies like 3d printing and Raspberry Pi. Either is good with me!

In my spare time, I enjoy making electronic video gamey music, consuming sci fi and fantasy works in all their various formats, and participating in larps and tabletop roleplaying games.
